# System Overview — OS4U SaaS Platform

The OS4U platform is an advanced, multi-tenant ERP and SaaS management system designed specifically for technical assistance businesses (smartphones, electronics, etc.).

## Architecture

The system follows a **multi-tenant** design, ensuring complete data isolation between different technical assistance stores. Each tenant (store) operates in its own logical context, with separate management of customers, orders, and finances.

## Core Pillars

- **Technical Assistance ERP Modules**:
  - Advanced Order of Service (OS) management.
  - Complete inventory tracking for parts and consumables.
  - Financial management (transactions, bank accounts, and reporting).
  - Integration with WhatsApp for automated status updates.

- **Smart AI & Automation (Smart Modules)**:
  - **SmartParts**: Automated quoting system connecting stores to suppliers.
  - **SmartDiagnostics**: Future-ready AI tools for diagnosing hardware issues.
  - **SmartPricing**: Automated calculation for service labor and part margins.

- **Admin SaaS Control Panel (Master Admin)**:
  - Global overview of platform health and revenue (MRR).
  - Centralized management of tenants (active, trials, suspended).
  - SaaS Plan and Subscription lifecycle management.
  - Intelligent insights engine for upselling and risk detection.
